Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book your services?

For weddings, I recommend booking 8-12 months in advance, especially for peak season dates (May-October). For portrait sessions, 4-6 weeks notice is usually sufficient, though more lead time is always better for securing your preferred date and time.

What is your payment policy?

For weddings and events, a 50% non-refundable retainer is required to secure your date, with the remaining balance due two weeks before the event. For portrait sessions, payment is due in full at the time of booking. I accept credit cards, bank transfers, and checks.

How long until I receive my photos?

Portrait sessions are typically delivered within 2 weeks. Weddings and larger events are delivered within 4-6 weeks. During peak season (summer and fall), delivery times may be slightly longer. You'll receive a online gallery where you can download high-resolution images and share with friends and family.

Do you travel for photography sessions?

Yes, I am available for travel both domestically and internationally. Travel fees vary depending on location and duration. For destinations within 50 miles of Boston, there is no additional travel fee. Please inquire for a custom quote for your destination.
